Thesis Overview

This study focuses on developing and implementing a LoRa-based wireless monitoring system for the drying process of Theobroma cacao. The system continuously tracks temperature, humidity, and moisture content to ensure efficient drying and maintain cacao bean quality. Real-time data is transmitted through a wireless sensor network and displayed in an intuitive monitoring dashboard for analysis and decision-making.
